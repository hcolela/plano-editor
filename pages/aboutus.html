About us

We are a self-managed and freely accessible digital art community, which means that we are a community that focuses on the idea of being able to practice art based on our own decisions without the eventual lack of financial resources to be able to do so being too important. For this we base ourselves on the idea of freedom in its broadest sense. The freedom to choose our path. We are not a community of professional photographers who travel the world with the most expensive equipment. We do not offer photographic trips or photographic ebooks of the most remote places on the planet, although we would like to do all of this in the future, today we are a community of access to the exercise of digital and photographic art showing that it is possible to access the wonderful artistic exercise even in the most unfavorable conditions, it is enough to make the decision. We are free to do it and we ourselves manage to make it possible based on daily decision-making. In this first part of the work we focus on the City of Buenos Aires, the work could be called Mysterious Buenos Aires, it is called a work of urban realism, we try to show you the contradictions of Buenos Aires, a city that is denied daily like this itself, a city that even its own inhabitants know little about, but that is truly mysterious.
